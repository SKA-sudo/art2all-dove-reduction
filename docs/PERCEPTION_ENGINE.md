Purpose

The Art2all Perception Engine does not generate artwork.

It discovers the minimal perceptual relationships required to preserve
stable human perception.

The engine exists to preserve the recognizability of the Peace Dove while
its visual complexity continuously grows.

Its purpose is not computational optimisation.

Its purpose is perceptual stability.

################################################################
Core Principle
################################################################

The engine never invents perception.

The engine discovers perception.

Every architectural decision must improve the understanding of human
perception before it improves software implementation.

################################################################
Perception State
################################################################

A Perception State represents the complete observable state of the
reference model at one specific moment.

It is independent from rendering implementation.

It represents what can currently be perceived.

Future Perception States may contain

- geometry

- pose

- behaviour

- semantic regions

- silhouette

- motion

- future observable perception information

Movement is not treated as animation.

Movement may represent semantic information.

################################################################
Perception Rules
################################################################

A Perception Rule describes one reproducible law of human perception.

Every rule

- originates from observation

- is experimentally validated

- remains implementation independent

Only validated rules become part of the Production Engine.
