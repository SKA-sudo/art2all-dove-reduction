# 1. Mission

## Purpose

The Reduction Lab is the scientific research environment of Art2all.

Its purpose is **not** to build product features or a general theory of
perception.

Its purpose is to discover the minimal perceptual and semantic
structures required for a human observer to perceive the Art2all Peace
Dove built from children's drawings.

Research exists to improve the Art2all product.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Central Research Question

> What is the minimal semantic relationship structure that must be
> preserved so that a human observer still perceives a beautiful Peace
> Dove although it is constructed entirely from children's drawings?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 2. Research Philosophy

## Observation precedes explanation

The Reduction Lab is an **observational perception laboratory**.

Research starts with observation of the completed reference model.

Hypotheses are formulated only after stable observations have been made.

The reference model already contains the solution.

Our task is to discover it.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Research Scope

Always reduce before expanding.

Solve one validated perceptual problem at a time.

Do not investigate new perception domains until the current reference
model requires them.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 3. Development Strategy

Phase 1
:   Static construction of the Art2all Peace Dove from children's
    drawings.

Phase 2
:   Validate perception during animation.

Phase 3
:   Introduce additional domains only when required (lighting, sleeping,
    interaction, etc.).

Engineering must remain extensible while solving today's validated
problem.

**Build for today's validated observations. Design for tomorrow's
discoveries.**
